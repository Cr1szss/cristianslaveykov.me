Welcome to my personal website!

Hello there, my name is Nyx or Nyxie. Im a transgender female and use 'she/they' pronouns. Additionally, I am an aspiring game developer with a strong belief in the importance of privacy, open-source software, and equality for all. I am proficient in HTML, C++, Golang, and GDScript. I also have a deep appreciation for anime, considering works like Serial Experiments Lain and Neon Genesis Evangelion as significant pieces of art. Furthermore, I am an avid user of GNU/Linux, specifically Arch Linux.

The main purpose of this website is to embrace the original purpose of the World Wide Web: a decentralized space where individuals have their own personal websites instead of relying on ~5 platforms to share their opinions. This website reflects my desktop environment, serving as my personal platform for discussing topics such as politics, game development, philosophy, and more. It employs the Nord theme, furthermore mirroring the aesthetic of my desktop setup.
